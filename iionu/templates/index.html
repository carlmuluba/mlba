THE iiONu PROJECT

Inspired by the Aye-aye from Madagascar - an endangered strepsirrhine primate, this project aims to make individuals aware of their responsibilities to the planet. If today the signs of decay that lead us to environmental disaster are evident, we also know that nature always survives.

As such, we are minting 10000 nfts at Polygon chain that have the eyes on each of us.

The collection is available at Opensea.
